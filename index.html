Mashathini (4IR) Foundation

"Empowering Futures, Transforming Lives: Nurturing Tech Innovators for a Digital Tomorrow"

  • Our vision

    to create a future where every young person has equal access to quality ICT education, enabling them to thrive as skilled professionals and entrepreneurs. We aspire to build a world where technology is a tool for empowerment and positive change.

  • Our Mission

    to empower youth from diverse backgrounds with cutting-edge ICT education, mentorship, and entrepreneurial resources. We aim to foster a generation of tech innovators equipped to lead in the digital age.

  • Our Values

    Innovation: We embrace innovation in our curriculum, mentorship approaches, and support initiatives, staying ahead of technological advancements to prepare our beneficiaries for the future. Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and inclusivity, providing equal opportunities for youth from all backgrounds to access our programs and services.

  • Our Values

    Empowerment: Empowering youth is at the heart of our foundation. We empower young minds with knowledge, skills, and confidence, enabling them to create meaningful change in their lives and communities.
